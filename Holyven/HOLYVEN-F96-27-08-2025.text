the National Assembly 2 	In these Rules, unless the context requires otherwise- Interpre-tation “Candidate” means a Member of Parliament who is duly nominated to stand for election to the Pan-African Pan-African Parliament is set to be conducted; “Nomination” means nomination as a Candidate to stand for election to the Pan-African Parliament; “Nomination day” means the day which shall by notice published by the Returning Officer, be set to be the last day for receiving application forms from Candidates; “Nominator” means a Member of Parliament of the Parliament of the United Republic of
